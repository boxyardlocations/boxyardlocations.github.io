A personal note · February 2026
Dear Friends & Former Patients,

As the years have graciously passed, I find myself in a quieter chapter of life. While my medical practice has closed its doors, I remain grateful for the connections and memories built over decades of service to this community.

At my age, I have stepped away from the digital world and phones, finding solace in the simpler rhythms of days gone by. However, I am still reachable through the time-honored tradition of written correspondence. There is something meaningful about a letter — it allows for thoughtful reflection on both ends.

My former office space may be quiet now, but the kind neighbors at the business next door have agreed to hold mail on my behalf. If you wish to reach me, please send your correspondence to the address below.

Dr. Rosenberg
Board-Certified Podiatric Surgeon · Pro-Israel Advocate
