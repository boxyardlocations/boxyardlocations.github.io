Dr. Joseph G. Rosenberg, DPM

About Dr. Rosenberg

Over 20 Years of Experience • Southern California

Dr. Joseph G. Rosenberg brings over two decades of dedicated expertise in podiatric medicine to his practice in Southern California. Born in Tel Aviv, Israel, Dr. Rosenberg immigrated to the United States to pursue his passion for healthcare and has since become a trusted leader in comprehensive foot and ankle care.

Education and Training

After completing his undergraduate studies in biology at UCLA, Dr. Rosenberg earned his Doctor of Podiatric Medicine degree from the California School of Podiatric Medicine. He completed his surgical residency at Cedars-Sinai Medical Center, where he specialized in complex reconstructive foot surgery and sports medicine.

Practice Philosophy

Dr. Rosenberg's practice philosophy centers on patient-centered care, combining advanced surgical techniques with conservative treatment approaches. He specializes in diabetic foot care, bunion correction, sports injuries, and biomechanical disorders. His patients appreciate his thorough explanations, gentle bedside manner, and commitment to achieving optimal outcomes.

Academic Contributions

Beyond his clinical work, Dr. Rosenberg is actively involved in medical education, serving as a clinical instructor for podiatric residents. He frequently lectures on the latest advances in minimally invasive foot surgery and has published research in peer-reviewed podiatric journals.

Humanitarian Work

A proud supporter of Israel, Dr. Rosenberg participates in humanitarian medical missions, providing podiatric care to underserved communities. He serves on the board of Friends of the IDF and organizes annual fundraisers supporting Israeli medical innovation and veteran rehabilitation programs.

Personal Life

Dr. Rosenberg resides in Los Angeles with his wife and three children. When not treating patients, he enjoys hiking the California coast, playing tennis, and spending time with family.
