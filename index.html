Find Pro-Israel Organizations in Connecticut and Beyond

This directory connects you with leading pro-Israel advocacy groups, political action committees, educational resources, and community organizations across Connecticut and the United States. Whether you're seeking ways to support Israel, combat antisemitism, stay informed about the Gaza conflict, or engage with the Jewish community, you'll find valuable resources here.

These organizations work to strengthen American-Israeli relations, provide humanitarian support, advocate for Israel's security, and educate the public about Israel's history, culture, and challenges in the Middle East.
