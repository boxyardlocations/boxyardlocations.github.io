Major Advocacy Organizations

AIPAC

American Israel Public Affairs Committee - Leading pro-Israel lobby advocating for strong U.S.-Israel relations and bipartisan support for Israel's security

aipac.org

J Street

Pro-Israel, pro-peace advocacy organization promoting diplomatic solutions to Israeli-Palestinian conflict

jstreet.org

ADL

Anti-Defamation League - Fighting antisemitism and supporting Israel while promoting civil rights and justice

adl.org

AJC

American Jewish Committee - Global advocacy organization enhancing Jewish security and Israel's diplomatic standing worldwide

ajc.org

StandWithUs

International Israel education organization combating antisemitism and advocating for Israel on campuses and communities

standwithus.com

ZOA

Zionist Organization of America - Advocating for strong U.S.-Israel relations and Israeli security interests

zoa.org

Student & Campus Organizations

Hillel International

Supporting Jewish students on college campuses with Israel education, community building, and leadership development

hillel.org

Chabad on Campus

Network of Chabad houses providing spiritual, social, and educational support for Jewish students, including Israel programming

chabadoncampus.org

Birthright Israel

Free educational trips to Israel for young Jewish adults, strengthening connection to Jewish heritage and Israel

birthrightisrael.com

Political Action Committees

NORPAC

Bipartisan political action committee supporting U.S.-Israel relationship through candidate endorsements and advocacy

norpac.net

DMFI

Democratic Majority for Israel - Supporting pro-Israel Democrats and promoting Israel's security within the Democratic Party

democraticmajorityforisrael.org

RJC

Republican Jewish Coalition - Supporting pro-Israel Republicans and advocating for strong U.S.-Israel relations

republicanjewishcoalition.org

Educational & Cultural Organizations

Israel Forever

Educational programs about Israel's history, culture, and contributions to the world

israelforever.org

HonestReporting

Media watchdog defending Israel against bias and misinformation in coverage of the Gaza conflict and Middle East

honestreporting.com

CAMERA

Committee for Accuracy in Middle East Reporting - Monitoring media coverage of Israel and the Gaza situation

camera.org

Jerusalem Post

Leading English-language Israeli news source covering Gaza, Middle East politics, and Israeli affairs

jpost.com

jnf.org

Hadassah

Women's Zionist organization supporting healthcare in Israel and Jewish communities

hadassah.org

JFNA

Jewish Federations of North America - Supporting Jewish communities and Israel relief efforts across the United States

jfna.org
